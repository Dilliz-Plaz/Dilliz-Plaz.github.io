Police Seek Witnesses in Downtown Assault Case

By Metro News Staff | May 19, 2025
Vancouver Police are appealing to the public for witnesses and information following a serious assault that occurred late Sunday night near Granville Street in downtown Vancouver.

According to authorities, the incident took place around 11:45 p.m. outside a popular nightclub. A 28-year-old man was found unconscious with significant injuries and was transported to hospital, where he remains in stable condition.

"We believe there were several people in the area at the time who may have witnessed the assault or have information that could help our investigation," said Sgt. Lisa Chen of the Vancouver Police Department.
Police have released surveillance images from nearby businesses and are urging anyone who may have seen suspicious activity or has dashcam footage from the area to come forward.

The suspect is described as a male, approximately 6 feet tall, wearing a dark hoodie and jeans. Investigators are canvassing the area for additional witnesses and video evidence.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Vancouver Police Department or Crime Stoppers anonymously.
