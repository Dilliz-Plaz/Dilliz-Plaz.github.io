Vancouver Residents Alarmed as Forest Fires Rage in Highrock Park

By Metro News Staff | Monday, May 19, 2025
Residents of Vancouver's west end are sounding the alarm as multiple forest fires have broken out in Highrock Park, leaving scorched earth and rising smoke—and people are frustrated with what they’re calling a lack of serious response from local police. Over the past week, two separate fires have been reported in the heavily wooded park. Though firefighters have worked around the clock to contain the flames, many locals feel the police haven’t done enough to investigate the cause. The public is wondering if it was arson or just plain luck

"I am incredibly worried about the safety of your amazing forests," said one local resident who asked not to be named. "The police have blamed the wrong individuals and really need to step up their plan, right now."
As of this morning, Vancouver Fire Rescue Services says the fires are “under control.” Officials have asked the public to be careful

Meanwhile, local leaders are calling for more funding for fire prevention, stronger park surveillance, and an independent review of how police have handled the situation.

MNN will keep following this story. For now, stay alert, stay safe, and remember: one spark is all it takes.
